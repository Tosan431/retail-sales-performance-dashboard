## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Retail Sales Performance Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze:

- Revenue performance
- Customer purchasing behaviour
- Product profitability
- Payment preferences
- Monthly profit trends
- Regional sales distribution

The dashboard was designed to provide business stakeholders with actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

## Key Insights

- Clothing contributed 63% of total product demand.
- Cash on Delivery (COD) remained the most preferred payment method.
- Maharashtra generated the highest regional revenue.
- Printers delivered the highest product segment profitability.
- Profitability declined significantly in July and December.

- KPI performance tracking
- Monthly profit trend analysis
- Product category demand analysis
- Customer payment preference insights
- Regional revenue analysis
- Product profitability analysis
- Interactive quarter and category filtering
